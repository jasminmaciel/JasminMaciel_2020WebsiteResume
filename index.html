I am a sophomore at UC Riverside majoring in Liberal Studies with an intent to earn my B.A. Following this degree, I will begin the process of applying to the Graduate School of Education at UCR to earn my M.S and my teaching certification in Multiple Subjects. Following this additional degree and certification, I will move forward and continue my education in order to also cerfity myself with a High School Counsleor credential.

I seek a career in the category of education that will allow me to both fufill the role as a teacher and counselor. I aspire to help young children and young adults reach their full educational potential while guiding them onto a successful path that is founded on organization, flexiblity, and communication. With the tools I have and the education I have recieved, I will be prepared and determined to assist any student that may need it.

My goal is to create and maintain a healthy balance of enhancing a student's educational future alongside a path that recognizes the importance of mental and physical well-being. From personal experience and from listening to the experience of others, I have leanred that individual well-being and eduaction are crucial for the development of one another. If a student's well-being is unbalanced than it effects their education. If a student's education is unbalanced than it effects their well-being. In understanding these pillars, I can not see how I, a voucher of education and a supporter of mental and physical health, could chose to pursue anything differently. What I am here to do is help students be the best they can inside and out--to help students build a foundation for themselves and use it to grow and prosper.
